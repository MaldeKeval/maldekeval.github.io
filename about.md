I am a telecom systems engineer with hands-on experience across 5G RAN software
development, system integration, and performance optimization. I enjoy building
proof-of-concepts and turning them into practical, measurable improvements in
real deployments.

## Background

- **Current role:** Senior Product Development Engineer (RAN Developer) at Wisig Networks, Hyderabad
- **Core domain:** MAC-layer features in C, RAN parameter tuning, and scheduler-focused optimization
- **Prior work:** 5G Testbed and research roles at IIT Hyderabad, including cloud RAN studies and protocol implementation

## Current work

My day-to-day work includes CPU/core/thread profiling, radio parameter
fine-tuning for deployment scenarios, and end-to-end issue analysis using
Wireshark, QXDM, and XCAL logs. I also use Python and shell scripts to automate
operational and analysis workflows.

## Research and publications

- **Understanding Energy Consumption of Cloud Radio Access Networks: An Experimental Study**

In my M.Tech research, I focused on cloud RAN performance across CPU frequency,
core allocation, cache behavior, and power consumption to identify practical
configurations that preserve KPIs under different traffic patterns.
